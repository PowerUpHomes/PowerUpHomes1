Victorian Energy Upgrades

What is the VEU Rebate?

The Victorian Energy Upgrades program offers point-of-sale discounts on eligible energy-efficient appliances — including high-efficiency split system air conditioners. The discount is applied directly to your installation cost by participating installers.

Available to eligible Victorian homeowners Discount applied at the point of sale — no paperwork for you.
Must use a registered installer Only VEU-accredited businesses can apply the discount — which is exactly who we connect you with.
Applies to high-efficiency models Eligible systems meet minimum star rating and energy efficiency standards.
